  answers to the first three questions can all be found with a single nmap 
command:

sudo nmap -A -p- -vv -oN "<output-filename>" <machine-ip>
-A  detect OS, software versions, apply the basic script set,provide a traceroute; 
    this switch is the equivalent of the combined -O -sV -sC --traceroute 
-p- scan all ports. 
-vv -requesting the very verbose mode of nmap
-oN <filename> option sends the results to a txt file.
